Internships

We would be providing internships to talented students to work with us in our Data Science Journey. Selected interns would be working with our experts of more than Two decades of industry experience on real world problems.

You would also be trained on how to participate in Data Science Competitions. The mode of working is through industry standard digital collaborative workspace using Slack. You would also be able to build a project report and a data science portfolio which can be show cased to future employers.
